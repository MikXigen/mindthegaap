Mind The Gaap

Musician • Producer • DJ

Absolute music geek focused on deep, minimalistic and melodic types of house and techno.

Since an early age had a fun out of anything that helped him making noise. Participated in many musical playing stuff from hip-hop to hard core and metal. Once discovered electronic music he fully got into it, firstly on the dance floor just to find himself in a DJ booth.

As a musician he naturally started making his own beats. An early productions he were using as a personal DJ tools. At some point he found that his music is ready to be shared with the World. Since then he worked with a number of well reputable record labels such as Global Underground or Natura Viva and released many successful tracks.
